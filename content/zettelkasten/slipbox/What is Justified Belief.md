[Goldman A. 1979. What is Justified Belief? ](https://www.andrew.cmu.edu/user/kk3n/epistclass/goldmanjust.pdf)

This is an explanatory theory for what makes a belief justified and unjustified. We are stating the substantive conditions in which a belief becomes justified and this should be stated in non-epistemic terms and should also be revelatory in some sense. 

This latter point tackles *why* a person is justified in believing (x).
