There are generally two ways. Cold Process or Hot Process

Cold Process 
Pros 
1. Easier to control the reaction
2. Much more moldable 

Cons
1. Takes Longer (4 - 6 weeks)

Hot Process
Pros
1. Takes a single day 
2. Has a rustic appearance. 

Cons
1. Designs are harder to implement
2. Can't use many ingredients
