Today I read about a whale with a century old harpoon stuck in it's eye, awfully rude nobody bothered to take it out. These days they say that that whales can speak by clicking their tongues very slowly, a language of tsk-tsks. I'd rather not believe it, when I was young my aunt would tsk at me whenever I got the slightest thing wrong. From this, I hurriedly came to the conclusion that she was a sad old women. Still, when a boy at school (I was very young) made the same noise I jumped on him immediately but couldn't find it in me to slap or punch him. To me, it's a wonder that these poor whales aren't constantly bruised up.
