The Father: 
The original character from Kannitverstan. 

When I was young my father told me about a voyage he made when he was young. He told me of tall roofs and large harbours, he spoke of a man with wealth, women, power, tall roofs, and large harbours, this man whose name I cannot remember, owned almost all of Amsterdamn, yet on the day of my fathers arrival he was laid under that same land that he ran like a king yesterday. 

-

He would never touch liquor, at events he would nurse a single beer. Old age demands filling, out of every pore flowed grease, sweat, and the same sweet alcohol, day after day, raising my mothers patience and deflating her purse.
