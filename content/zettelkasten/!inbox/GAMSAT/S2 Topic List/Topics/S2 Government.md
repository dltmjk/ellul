Government
Themes: Impact of government, ethics of governance, individual freedom vs collective responsibility. The rights of government 

Motivation: The government is never really for the people, but instead represents the interests of the class which formed it. 

Before the french revolution, the french nation represented the interests of the aristocracy. 

Thesis: The government as a tool of the ruling class to maintain power and capital. The shifting of government from one political system to another in order to maintain class interests. Power doesn’t corrupt, those with power seek to maintain their interests.
