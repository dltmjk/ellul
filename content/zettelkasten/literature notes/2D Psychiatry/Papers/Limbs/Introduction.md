characterizing the normal and the pathological. Attention has shifted between psychi-
atric kinds (Haslam, 2002; Kendler et al., 2011; Zachar 2000, 2015), the conceptual
analysis of demarcation criteria (Boorse, 1977; Cummins, 1975; Engelhardt, 1975;
Nordenfelt, 2006), pragmatic (De Vreese, 2017), gradualist (Hauswald, 2016) and
anticonceptual approaches (Hesslow, 1993; Kincaid, 2008). However, as pointed out
by Kathryn Tabb (Tabb, 2019), most of these approaches have focused on diagnos-
