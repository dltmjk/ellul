## Image of Objectivity Paper
1. D&G trace a history of Objectivity using scientific atlases.
	1. Why do they use atlases? 
		1. They were manifestos. They standardized the subjects and were used in teaching the new practitioners of the subject. 
2. Generally their lineage of ideals goes something like 
	1. Truth to Nature $\rightarrow$ Mechanical Objectivity $\rightarrow$ Trained Judgement
		1. Each were aiming at "what truly is"
	2. Where each represents a set of epistemic virtues with their own histories. It's important to note that they don't seamlessly transition from one to another. These virtues co-exist and are changed through the introducbtion of newer ones. 
	> 	"Seventeenth-century epistemology aspired to the viewpoint of angels; nineteenth-century objectivity aspired to the self discipline of saints." Image of Objectivity 
	3. They argue that mechanical objectivity popularized a certain type of scientific character. A person with "infinite patience, unflagging perseverance, preternatural sensory acuity, and an insatiable appetite for work."
