[[Zettelkasten]]

Zettelkasten notetaking can be enhanced with the following categories of Notes/Folders. 

Fleeting Notes
1. Notes you take on the fly, For example:
	1. Quotes from a book
	2. Ideas that you have sometimes. 
	3. Stuff that is interesting that you might want to refer to 

Literature Notes
1. Condensed notes of an Article/Book/Etc
	1. Summaries of papers
	2. Usually re-written as to ensure understanding 

Permanent Notes
1. Notes summarizing a single idea that are assigned keywords. 
	1. Notes that represent core ideas/topics. 
	2. Can be understood without context. 

Types of Folders
1. Inbox
	1. This is where you put fleeting notes that need to be processed. It contains thoughts, recommendations, other highlights that make for a fleeting note. You need to regularly process the contents of your inbox. 
2. Someday
	1. Things that are interesting but you don't have the time or resources to pursue right now. This folder is to remind you of these things, ideas that are promising. 
3. Raw
	1. Exported highlights from books: such as quotes and other stuff.
