When we consider things in the order that corresponds to our knowledge of them, our view of them must be different from what it would be if we were speaking of them in accordance with how they exist in reality. ([A]liter spectandas esse res singulas in ordine ad cognitionem nostram, quàm si de ijsdem loquamur prout revera existunt.)
